Nowadays IFS+ is a comprehensive software solution that allows to manage brokerage activities on the Forex market. The purpose of the software product is to automate full workflow of backoffice, sales, marketing, risk management and other departments involved in brokerage.

IFS+ provides integration with trade platforms such as Metatrader, Ctrader etc. IFS+ allows you to control both client area and backoffice operations. The most important advantage of IFS+ is an integrated CRM solution which simplifies the workflows and doesn’t require integration with external CRM.

Also IFS+ provides management of multi-level IB programs and gives a possibility to set and charge a commission to partners in a flexible and unlimited way.

IFS+ CRM features

Integrated workspace for CRM tools and Backoffice

Now any Backoffice user can use the CRM functionality. To make a comment, to assign an event or add information can be done by a couple of clicks.

IFS+ using customers ranking scheme widely spread among brokers:

Demo Leads, Live Leads and Clients. You may also catch and track your visitors who are not ready yet to become a client with Simple Leads rank functionality. And of course IFS+ provides powerful tool to work with tour Partners and Ibs.

Highlight your customers with colors to simplify and speed up the processes of interaction with the customers. Time is money.

The possibility of replacement of one sales by another one.

Now your employees can easily go on vacation. In this case there is no need to reassign the customers of the employee departed; just set up substitution and the substitute employee will see all the details of the customers and tasks.
